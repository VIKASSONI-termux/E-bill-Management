KEY COMPONENTS:
===============

1. COOLING TOWER: External heat dissipation system
2. CDU: Central control unit managing coolant flow
3. COLD PLATES: Direct contact cooling for hot components
4. COOLANT PIPES: Circulating fluid carrying heat away
5. SERVER UNITS: Individual servers with liquid-cooled components

COOLANT FLOW:
=============
Coolant flows from CDU → Server Units → Cold Plates → Back to CDU → Cooling Tower

BENEFITS:
=========
- Higher cooling efficiency than air cooling
- Reduced noise levels
- Better temperature control
- Higher component density possible
- Lower power consumption for cooling
